🛠 Coping Strategies for Imposter Syndrome

  • 1. Talk about it: Share your feelings with trusted friends or mentors. You're not alone.
  • 2. Track your wins: Keep a success journal. Write down even small achievements.
  • 3. Challenge negative thoughts: Replace “I’m not good enough” with “I’m learning and growing.”
  • 4. Avoid comparisons: Everyone has a unique path. Focus on yours.
  • 5. Accept compliments: Practice saying “Thank you” instead of deflecting praise.
  • 6. Understand it’s normal: Even the most successful people feel like imposters sometimes.
  • 7. Set realistic goals: Don’t aim for perfection—aim for progress.
  • 8. Celebrate growth: Reflect on how far you’ve come, not just how far you have to go.
